# PROTEIN-FOLD-

An Interactive Computational Pipeline Linking Macromolecular Misfolding Constraints with Genome-Wide Selection Filters


🚀 Live Web Application: https://sqezcbzwtteoysrs2tgv8e.streamlit.app/


🔬 Scientific & Project Background
Genes and proteins rarely act in isolation. During evolutionary timelines, amino acid sequences accumulate mutations, generating genetic diversity. These mutations are subject to the stringent filtering of natural selection.

Inspired by the Protein Misfolding Avoidance Hypothesis, Evo-Fold bridges dry-lab structural parameters with evolutionary sequence metrics. The core objective is to understand why different genes and structural regions are subject to different evolutionary forces (purifying and positive selection).

Core Research Alignment: Highly expressed proteins face severe selective pressure to fold accurately and avoid toxic cellular misfolding aggregates. This platform evaluates how spatial localization and thermodynamic stability translate into evolutionary conservation.
